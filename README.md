## Overview

Continuum is an autonomous engineering workspace designed to unify and streamline your software development lifecycle. By seamlessly connecting your repositories and coordinating fleets of AI agents, Continuum enables your engineering team to review production fixes and make critical release decisions—all within a single, elegantly composed interface.

## Key Capabilities

### 🤖 Autonomous Agent Coordination
Deploy and manage a specialized fleet of AI agents working tirelessly in the background. Whether it's patching memory leaks, generating regression tests, or migrating middleware, Continuum's agents proactively identify and resolve complex engineering challenges.

### 📊 Unified Engineering Intelligence
Make informed decisions with real-time analytics. Monitor vital metrics such as:
- **Engineering Health & Code Quality**
- **Agent Confidence & Throughput**
- **Repository Indexing & Health Scores**
- **Pending Approvals & Open Reviews**

### 🛡️ Enterprise-Grade Security
Built for scale and security from day one:
- **Private by Design:** Your proprietary code and architecture data remain strictly confidential.
- **SOC 2 Ready:** Engineered to comply with rigorous enterprise security and auditing standards.
- **Enterprise SSO:** Seamless and secure identity management for your entire organization.

### ⚡ Seamless Repository Integration
Connect instantly to your existing infrastructure. Continuum deeply indexes your codebases—whether they're built in TypeScript, Python, Go, or Rust—providing structural awareness and instant architectural mapping.

## Why Continuum?

Modern engineering teams suffer from tool fatigue and scattered context. Continuum replaces fragmented workflows with a cohesive command center. Instead of hunting down bugs across multiple platforms, Continuum brings the issues, the context, and the AI-generated solutions directly to you for a streamlined, one-click final approval.

You maintain complete control. Continuum provides the autonomous leverage.
